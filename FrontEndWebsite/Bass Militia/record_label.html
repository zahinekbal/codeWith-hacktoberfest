Teotek - Maglev EP

[November 26th 2019]

Bass Militia proudly present the Maglev EP from Teotek. If you haven't heard of Teotek before, you are in for a treat.

With releases on labels such as SWB Records, In:flux Audio & 3000Digital, he has already established himself as a reputable producer.

Maglev and Synthetic execute the high tech aesthetic in audio form, pushing 4x4 bass music into new territory with forward thinking sound design contorted into hard mechanical rhythms, injections of precision micro edits and glitched out soundscapes.

[May 8th 2019]

Welcome to Bass Militia Records first official EP!

Leading the attack is Martial Taktics with his original track 'Fight'. Martial Taktics has been making a lot of noise in the scene in the past year and his track just proves what all the fuss is about! On the remix duties we have some incredible work from Mutant Bass creator and legend, Kanji Kinetic. You know if he's on a track it means business and F**K how he has delivered!? Who's Peal you ask? Peal remixed this original track and is an up and coming talent on the scene. Some insane production skills from the man. Peal is definitely one to watch in the future. Maybe you will be seeing more of him through us, but we wouldn't be able to confirm that ;) Amen Shamen has earned respect in the rave and club community by producing on point mash ups of Jungle and Hardtek.
